Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to delete your account and associated data from Sellway, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to support@sellway.com with the subject "Delete My Account".
  2. Include your registered email address and username in the email.
  3. Our team will verify your request and delete your data within 7 working days.

For users who have connected via Facebook Login, you may also remove our app by visiting your Facebook App Settings and deleting the Sellway integration.
